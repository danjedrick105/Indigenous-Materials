USES AND APPLICATION

Decorative Wood Products

Beyond structural and functional uses, coco lumber is widely applied in decorative wood products and interior design accents. It is used in carved panels, ceiling details, moldings, wall features, and ornamental elements that enhance the visual appeal of spaces. Designers appreciate coco lumber for its unique texture and color variation, which cannot be replicated by synthetic materials. Decorative items made from coco lumber allow homeowners and architects to incorporate natural and locally sourced materials into their designs, reinforcing themes of sustainability, craftsmanship, and cultural identity.
