USES AND APPLICATION

Flooring, Wall Panels, and Ceilings

Coco lumber is increasingly used for flooring and wall paneling due to its distinctive grain pattern and natural aesthetic. The wood displays unique linear textures and contrasting shades that add visual depth and character to interior spaces. Medium-density coco lumber is ideal for flooring applications because it balances strength and workability, making it resistant to everyday wear when properly finished. For wall panels, coco lumber provides a warm, organic appearance that enhances both modern and traditional interiors. It also contributes to thermal comfort by helping regulate indoor temperature, making living spaces more comfortable and energy-efficient.
